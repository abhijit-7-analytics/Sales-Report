# 📊 Sales Performance Analytics Dashboard

An interactive **Power BI dashboard project** analysing the sales performance of **DJ Limited** from **2023–2025**.  
This project transforms raw sales data into meaningful insights using **data visualization and business intelligence techniques**.

---

# 📌 Project Overview

The goal of this project is to analyse company sales data to understand:

- Product performance
- Customer purchasing behaviour
- Sales channel contribution
- Regional sales trends
- Monthly and yearly revenue growth

The project demonstrates how **data analytics supports business decision-making through interactive dashboards.**

---

# 🎯 Project Objective

- Analyse sales trends and patterns from historical data
- Identify high-performing products and categories
- Compare **online vs retail sales channels**
- Study **customer segmentation (Gold vs Regular)**
- Convert raw data into **interactive insights using Power BI**

# 📊 Key Analysis Performed

## 1️⃣ Monthly Sales Trend
- Analysed monthly revenue patterns.
- Identified fluctuations and peak sales months.

## 2️⃣ Product Performance

Products analysed:

- Printer
- Tablet
- Mobile
- Laptop
- Headphones

Comparison between **Total Sales and Average Unit Price**.

## 3️⃣ Customer Type Analysis

Customer segments:

- Gold Customers
- Regular Customers

Revenue contribution is almost equal between both groups.

## 4️⃣ Sales Channel Analysis

Sales channels include:

- Online
- Retail

Both channels contribute almost **50% each**, showing strong omnichannel sales.

## 5️⃣ Category Analysis

Categories analysed:

- Accessories
- Electronics
- Gadgets
- Home
- Office

Helps identify product demand across categories.

# 💡 Key Business Insights

- Sales show **consistent growth between 2023–2025**.
- Revenue contribution from **Gold and Regular customers is balanced**.
- **Online and retail channels contribute equally**, indicating effective sales distribution.
- Product-level insights help identify **high-performing items for marketing focus**.
- Monthly patterns reveal opportunities for **seasonal promotions**.
